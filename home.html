FAQ

1. What types of goods are available for sale?

Our market offers a wide variety of goods, including poultry, meat, fish, boutique items (such as clothes and shoes), groceries, fruits, vegetables, frozen goods, pharmacy items, coconuts, rice, toys, and canteen fare, ensuring there's something for everyone.

2. What are the operating hours?

The operating hours of the Karuhatan Public Market are from 4 AM to 9 PM, seven days a week.

3. Do vendors accept e-payment, or is it cash-only?

Vendors in the Karuhatan Public Market may vary in their payment methods, but many still primarily accept cash payments only. However, some vendors may also offer e-payment options through G-Cash, particularly in Phase 4, where all stalls are equipped with this payment method.
